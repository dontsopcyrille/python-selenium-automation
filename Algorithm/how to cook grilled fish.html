How to cook Grilled Fish

Ingredients:
  • 1 medium onion, finely grated
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
  • 2 cloves of garlic, grated
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 stock cube or Maggi cube
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper or white pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Half teaspoon crushed chilli or to taste
  • Salt
Instructions:

Grattez, Lavez et nettoyer votre poisson.

Scrape, wash and clean your fish.

1. use your knife to make some space between the fish.

2. Then coat the fish with really good marinade.

3. Put the marinade everywhere, even inside the fish.

4. Let the fish marinate for 1 hour or overnight in your fridge.

5. Preheat the barbecue.

6. grill the fish on the barbecue for 30 min, about 15 min on each side.
